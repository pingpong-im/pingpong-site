WHAT IS PINGPONG?

PingPong is a platform, which allows new way of initiating and managing the growth of any start-up or a team. It allows to communicate, collaborate and develop every aspect of your start-up and pay for completed work with crypto-currency.
The base functionality to issue your own token gives startupers an ability to involve people into their start-up by either selling tokens or award tokens to freelancers or think-alikes for completing tasks.
